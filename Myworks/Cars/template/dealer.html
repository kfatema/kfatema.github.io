Northland Nissan belongs to the AutoCanada family of dealerships. AutoCanada is one of Canada's largest multi-location automobile dealership groups, currently operating 24 franchised dealerships in British Columbia, Alberta, Manitoba, Ontario, New Brunswick and Nova Scotia. In 2010, our dealerships sold approximately 24,000 vehicles and processed approximately 317,000 service and collision repair orders in our 339 service bays. Currently AutoCanada sells the following new vehicle brands: Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge, FIAT, Nissan, Infiniti, Hyundai, Mitsubishi, Subaru and Volkswagen.
